**3. What is "Lorem Ipsum"?**
You will see "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet..." in many design templates. It is **placeholder text**. It is used because the letter distribution is similar to English, making it look like real sentences, but it has no actual meaning. This allows designers to focus on the layout (fonts, margins, and colors) rather than the words.
