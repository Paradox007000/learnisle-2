**PDFObject Testing**
This document serves as a functional sample to ensure that the PDFObject software is displaying pages and content correctly across a three-page format.

**Perception of Length**
The author digresses to discuss the relativity of length. Even though minutes and page sizes are technically constant, the text explores how they can be perceived as "long" or "short" (optimism vs. reality).
